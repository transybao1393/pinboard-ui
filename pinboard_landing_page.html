Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All data is stored locally on your device with end-to-end encryption. We never have access to your clipboard or files.

Which macOS versions are supported?

Smart Scratchpad works on macOS 12.0 (Monterey) and later, including macOS 13 (Ventura) and macOS 14 (Sonoma).

Can I customize keyboard shortcuts?

Yes! All keyboard shortcuts are fully customizable in Settings. Set them to whatever feels most natural for your workflow.

Does it work offline?

Yes, Smart Scratchpad works completely offline. Cloud sync is optional and only used if you enable iCloud integration.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free gives you 9 clipboard slots and basic features. Pro unlocks unlimited slots, iCloud sync, advanced file management, and custom themes.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact support for a full refund, no questions asked.

How much storage does it use?

The app itself is only 50MB. Clipboard history uses minimal space (typically under 100MB). You control file storage limits in Settings.

Can I use it on multiple Macs?

Yes! Pro and Teams licenses work on all your Macs. Enable iCloud sync to keep clipboard history in sync across devices.
